# veo-prompts.md — ystack ⚡

> **Example**: Video prompts (Veo) for marketing high-end AI software.

This is the specialized prompt library for generating cinematic, high-quality promotional videos for the **ystack** swarm system and its products.

---

## ── Cinematic Video Prompts 🎥

### 1. Minimal Product Reveal
- **Prompt**: Cinematic, close-up shot of a dark-themed computer screen with a blurred glassmorphism overlay. A sleek AI interface glows with a turquoise pulsing light. Smoke-like particles drift across the screen. 8K, hyper-realistic, slow-motion.
- **Tone**: Calm, premium, high-tech.

### 2. Multi-Agent Swarm Visualization
- **Prompt**: A futuristic digital network landscape. Seven glowing nodes, each with a unique icon (gear, brush, shield, etc.), pulse synchronously. Data streams flow between the nodes, converging toward a central hub with the ystack logo. Cyberpunk aesthetic, purple and blue neon, volumetric lighting.
- **Tone**: Powerful, collaborative, autonomous.

### 3. Privacy First Theme
- **Prompt**: A close-up of a local server rack in a dimly lit, modern apartment. A soft white light pulses on the server as it processes data offline. A window shows the rainy city outside. Macro shot, shallow depth of field, high contrast.
- **Tone**: Secure, local-first, trustworthy.
